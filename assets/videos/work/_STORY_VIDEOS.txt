Work-card "Quick look" story videos
===================================
Each work card's story circle opens a 10s Instagram/Snapchat-style
popup. Drop a vertical (9:16) MP4 here named by project id:

  assets/videos/work/ibp.mp4
  assets/videos/work/nqlah.mp4
  assets/videos/work/nitaq.mp4
  assets/videos/work/iwork.mp4

Until a video exists the popup shows the project screenshot (poster)
for 10s. Recommended: ~1080x1920, <10s, H.264 MP4.
