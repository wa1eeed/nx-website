PropTech · Auctions

Live bidding engine

Concurrency-safe auctions, zero double-bookings

The heart of the platform: an event-sourced bidding and reservation engine where thousands of bids settle in real time without a single double-booking. Every bid, hold and award is an immutable event, giving a complete, auditable trail and instant rollback safety.

  • Event-sourced bids with optimistic concurrency control
  • Real-time hold/award with atomic reservation
  • Full audit trail and replay for every auction
  • Anti-sniping and fair-close rules

PropTech · Ejar

Contracts & documentation

From winning bid to a signed, compliant contract

When a bid wins, the platform generates the contract, routes it for e-signature, and pushes the tenancy to Ejar, no re-keying, no parallel spreadsheets. KYC, ownership and disclosure checks are wired into the flow so nothing reaches signature without the required documents.

  • Auto-generated contracts from auction outcomes
  • Ejar tenancy registration and status sync
  • KYC and ownership verification in-flow
  • E-signature with a complete document trail
