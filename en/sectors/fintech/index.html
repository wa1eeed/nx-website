The challenge

What's blocking your financial product launch?

Three obstacles we see in almost every project, and how we handle them.

Obstacle one

Banking-integration complexity

Every bank has a different interface, every integration eats months of dev time, and maintenance is a permanent burden after launch.

Our fix

One interface through our licensed partners connects you to 30+ banks, and we own the integration, operation and maintenance.

Obstacle two

Compliance & licensing burden

Banking activity is regulated, and entering it directly means licences, capital and ongoing supervision most models can't carry.

Our fix

Regulated activity runs exclusively through central-bank-licensed partners, so you get the capability without holding the licence.

Obstacle three

The API-to-product gap

An API alone isn't a product: the experience, decision engine, ops dashboards and reporting are still on you.

Our fix

We build the full product around those APIs: app, decision engine, ops dashboard and reports ready for your team.

FAQ

FinTech with NX, answered

Who holds the licence, you or us?

Regulated banking activity runs through infrastructure partners licensed by the central bank; we build your product and wrap those capabilities inside it, so you get the function without carrying the licence burden. And if you later want your own licence, we build to match its requirements.

Do you connect us to every bank?

We connect you to the banks available through the open-banking ecosystem and our licensed partners, including the Kingdom's major banks (30+), with wider coverage across the Gulf depending on the case.

How long until launch?

A proof of concept takes 4 to 6 weeks, and a complete, launch-ready product 8 to 16 weeks, depending on scope and number of integrations.

Who owns the code and IP?

You do, entirely. You get full ownership of the code and intellectual property of the product we build.

How is customer data handled (PDPL)?

Consent-first: no data access without the customer's explicit consent through their bank's channels, with encryption in transit and at rest, scoped access controls, and processing compliant with the Personal Data Protection Law.

Can we start before getting a licence?

Yes. Many projects start through our licensed partners, so you launch your product without waiting for your own licence, and we prepare the architecture to match its requirements if you decide to obtain it later.
