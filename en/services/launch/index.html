Common questions

NX Launch, answered

Does this include both the website and the admin platform?

Yes. We build your full digital presence: an official website plus an admin platform to run your orders, customers and daily operations, wired together as one system.

How long does the launch take?

We agree on a clear schedule based on your size and launch in fast, sequential phases, starting with what matters most so you go live early instead of waiting for everything at once.

Are the systems off-the-shelf or built for my business?

Built for your needs. We understand how you work first, then shape the platform and automation around it, instead of forcing you into a generic tool.

What about government integration and compliance (SAMA, ZATCA, Nafath)?

That is an optional later phase we provide when you are ready, through NX Connect, wiring your platform into government systems and standards to elevate your brand and efficiency as you need it.

Do I fully own the website and system?

Yes, you own everything: the website, the platform and the data, documented and ready for you to extend or move whenever you want.
