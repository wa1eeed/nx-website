Common questions

NX 360, answered

We use a mix of systems, can you connect all of them?

That's the norm, not the exception. We integrate ERPs, CRMs, accounting tools, payment gateways and government endpoints, and where a system has no clean API, we engineer a reliable bridge rather than forcing you to replace it.

Is our ZATCA e-invoicing actually compliant?

We implement ZATCA Phase 2 (Integration) requirements end to end, cryptographic stamping, clearance and reporting, so invoices are issued compliant and cleared automatically, with no manual rejections to chase.

What happens if an integration goes down?

Resilience is the point. Automations are built with retries, queues and fallback handling so a temporary outage in one system doesn't lose data or stop the business, the work resumes automatically when the connection recovers.

How quickly do we see value?

We sequence the highest-friction workflow first, often invoicing or approvals, so you feel the impact within the first few weeks, then expand coverage from there.
