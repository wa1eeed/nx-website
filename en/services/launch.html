Common questions

NX Launch, answered

Isn't building it "properly" slower than a quick prototype?

Up front, marginally. Over the life of the company, dramatically faster. A throwaway prototype costs you a full rewrite the moment you raise or scale, usually right when you can least afford the delay. NX Launch ships in 60-90 days with a foundation you keep.

We're pre-revenue. Do we really need compliance now?

If you operate in fintech, health, insurance or any regulated vertical, yes. Retrofitting NCA ECC and PDPL compliance after the fact is far more expensive than building it in, and missing it can block your first institutional round entirely.

Do we own the code and IP?

Completely. You receive full ownership of the codebase, infrastructure and documentation, plus an IP register clarifying every component and license, exactly what investors verify during diligence.

What happens after the MVP ships?

You can take it in-house, the documentation and pipeline are built for exactly that, or continue with NX Grow for scaling and NX Connect for deeper regulatory integration as you grow.
