Common questions

NX Connect, answered

Do you just advise, or do you build the integration?

We build it. NX Connect is engineering, not consulting, we deliver a working, tested integration in your production environment and stay accountable through certification and go-live, rather than handing you a report and walking away.

We already have a platform. Can you add these integrations to it?

Yes. NX Connect works on existing systems as well as new ones. We integrate into your current architecture, and if the codebase needs hardening first, we'll flag that honestly, often pairing this with NX Grow.

How long does a typical integration take?

It depends on the gateway and your current state. A single well-defined integration like ZATCA or Nafath is often weeks; a full multi-gateway financial stack with SAMA sandbox onboarding takes longer. We scope it precisely before committing to a timeline.

Will this pass a regulator's or auditor's review?

That's the standard we build to. Each integration ships with the compliance evidence and audit trails regulators and auditors expect, engineered in from the start, not reconstructed after the fact.
