Compliance & questions

Regulation, privacy and real value, answered plainly.

Is crypto trading legal in Saudi Arabia?

We don’t build trading or exchange products. We build blockchain platforms for tokenization, traceability, smart contracts and identity. Where an activity needs a licence, the digital-assets framework is evolving under CMA/SAMA, and we guide you to the required approvals.

Public or private blockchain?

For most enterprises we use permissioned or hybrid chains, you keep control, privacy and performance, and use public networks only where they genuinely add value.

Do you hold our funds or assets?

No. We build the platform you own and operate, and integrate licensed custody and payment providers. We don’t take custody ourselves.

How is personal data handled (PDPL)?

Personal data stays off-chain and PDPL-compliant; the chain stores only hashes and proofs, so records are verifiable without exposing private data.

Isn’t this just hype?

We only propose blockchain when it removes a real cost or risk, fraud, disputes, manual reconciliation, slow settlement. If a normal database is the better fit, we’ll tell you.

Can it connect to our systems and approvals?

Yes. We deliver the technical integration with your ERP, payments and government gateways, and route your application to the approvals each authority requires.
