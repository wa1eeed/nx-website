Compliance & questions

Payments, e-invoicing and data, answered for the Saudi market.

Which payment methods can customers use?

Mada, Apple Pay and cards through a SAMA-licensed payment gateway (Moyasar, HyperPay, Tap, etc.). Deposits and full prepayment are both supported.

Do receipts comply with ZATCA e-invoicing?

Yes. When you bill through the platform, receipts and invoices are issued as ZATCA-compliant (Fatoorah) e-invoices with QR codes.

Is customer data handled under PDPL?

Yes. Personal data is stored and processed under the Saudi PDPL, consent, access controls, retention limits and the right to deletion.

Can we charge no-show or cancellation fees?

Yes. Configurable deposit, cancellation-window and no-show rules are built in, with clear terms shown at the time of booking.

Does it support multiple branches and languages?

Yes. Unlimited branches, staff and resources, with full Arabic/English (RTL) interfaces for both customers and staff.

Can it integrate with our existing tools?

Yes. WhatsApp, SMS providers, payment gateways, Google/Apple calendars and your accounting or CRM can all be connected.
