Compliance & questions

ZATCA, PDPL, GOSI and integrations, answered for the Saudi market.

Does it meet ZATCA Phase 2 (Fatoorah) e-invoicing?

Yes. Invoicing is built to ZATCA Phase 2, integrated generation, QR codes, cryptographic stamping and clearance/reporting with the Fatoorah platform.

Where is our data stored (PDPL / residency)?

We host within the Kingdom where required and handle data under the Saudi PDPL, with role-based access, encryption and audit logging.

Can payroll handle GOSI and WPS/Mudad?

Yes. Payroll supports GOSI contributions and WPS/Mudad wage-protection files, plus end-of-service calculations.

Can it integrate with our current accounting or tools?

Yes. We either migrate you fully or build an integration layer so your existing accounting, POS or CRM keep working behind one system.

How is access controlled across departments?

Granular role-based permissions and approval workflows, so finance, HR and ops each see and do exactly what they should, with a full audit trail.

Off-the-shelf or custom, which is right for us?

It depends on how unique your workflow is. We start with a short audit and recommend adapted, modular or fully custom, with no upselling.
